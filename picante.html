i am happy to share the recipe although unfortunately cant provide you with exact quantities, i havent gotten around to measuring it out so i usually just eyeball it and adjust to taste

makes bottles
start with about pounds o hot peppers,

any type will do, i ve done it with jalapenos, serranos, cherry bombers, habaneros, or a blend o any o those,
but really whatever ya got around or is easy to get works

cut the tops off the peppers n throw em in a food processor or blender or chop em up real tiny if machines arent yer bag, include all the seeds!

peel whole heads of garlic
and add it to this, then grind it all up real nice

then add some apple cider vinegar to the chunky mush,
i would say start with about tablespoons
and you can add more later if it needs more o that vinegary bite

next add about bunch o cilantro,

and onions
if its a big one or a whole onion if its small-like and mix it up again

then add about cans
of crushed tomatoes
and teaspoons
o both cumin and smoked paprika (if u dont have smoked paprika, chipotle powder also works and adds a little extra spice!)
thats about it, adjust all of these to taste at this point to get it balanced how you like it.
sometimes i add a little honey and salt depending on how sweet or salty the canned tomatoes were
or a little cayenne pepper if its not spicy enough
it usually takes a few rounds of tasting and adjustin after everythins in there to get it how you like it
but this is fun because you can bust out the chips and have everyone try it and suggest what to add more of
